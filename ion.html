I O N

"Ion uses its energy management abilities to divert power between its three weapons systems."

Based on the Atlas class chassis, the Northstar is all about power and precision. With a central energy system that fuels all of its offensive and defensive abilities, Ion wears a shoulder-mounted laser, drops laser-triggered mines and can fire an oversized laser from the center of her chest.

ORDNANCE: LASER SHOT

Shoulder-mounted, this lethal beam cuts through anything in its way.

TACTICAL: TRIPWIRE

Laser-triggered explosive mines.

DEFENSIVE: VORTEX SHIELD

Absorb incoming fire and redirect it back at the enemy.

CORE: LASER CORE

A deadly oversized laser beam fired from the chest of the Ion and capable of stopping anything it its path.
