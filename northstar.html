N O R T H S T A R

"Northstar is a master of both flight and precision kills, A sniper who can fly."

Based on the Stryder class chassis, the Northstar is a master of flight and precision kills. Equipped with a chargeable sniper Plasma Railgun, Northstar seeks to pick off enemy Titans from afar; dealing high damage per shot or outright killing weakened opponents.

ORDNANCE: CLUSTER MISSILE

Creates sustained explosions on impact.

TACTICAL: VTOL HOVER

Vertical take-off hover.

DEFENSIVE: TETHER TRAP

Mines that lockdown nearby enemy titans.

CORE: FLIGHT CORE

Hover, unleashing rockets at targets below.
