L E G I O N

"Legion utilizes an assortment of ballistic abilities focusing on defense and control."

Based on the Ogre class chassis, the Legion is equipped with a powerful Predator minigun and a durable gunshield. The Legion provides powerful battlefield presence at all ranges with its superior firepower.

ORDNANCE: POWER SHOT

Close Range: Knocks back nearby enemies.
Long Range: Damages all enemies in its path.

TACTICAL: MODE SWITCH

Switch between close range and long range precision rounds.

DEFENSIVE: GUN SHIELD

Deploys a protective shield from the Predator Cannon.

CORE: SMART CORE

The Predator Cannon automatically locks onto anything in range.
