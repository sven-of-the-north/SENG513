R O N I N

"Ronin loves getting up close with its samurai vibe, and quick to get in and out tactics."

Based on the Stryder class chassis, the Ronin is a hit-and-run specialist. The Ronin is capable of high burst damage and high mobility, but lacks the durability for extended engagements. A combination of high speed and utility from his Phase Dash and Arc Wave give him the tools to get into and out of any fight.

ORDNANCE: ARC WAVE

Swiping his sword across the ground, Ronin creates an electric wave that damages and slows enemies hit by it.

TACTICAL: PHASE DASH

Ronin dashes in a given direction, temporarily phasing out of the world, avoid incoming fire and creating the element of surprise.

DEFENSIVE: SWORD BLOCK

Reduces damage taken. Can be held indefinitely.

CORE: SWORD CORE

Empowers his melee and sword abilities, and gives access to new sword attacks.
